# Decision Tracking

Capture key decisions made during your meetings.

## What You Get

```markdown
## Decisions
- Prioritize API v2 migration over new features
- Maintain backward compatibility through Q1
- Allocate 2 additional engineers to the migration team
- Launch target: March 15th
```

## How It Works

The AI identifies explicit decisions, agreements, and conclusions from your meeting discussion.

## Configuration

**Settings → MeetingMind → AI Enrichment**

- Enable **Extract decisions**

## Tips

- Works best when decisions are stated clearly in meetings
- "We decided...", "Let's go with...", "The plan is..." trigger detection
- Ambiguous discussions may not be captured as decisions
