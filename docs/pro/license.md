# License Activation

Activate your MeetingMind Pro license to unlock AI features.

## Get a License

1. Go to [tumbucon.gumroad.com/l/meetingmind-pro](https://tumbucon.gumroad.com/l/meetingmind-pro)
2. Complete your purchase ($39 one-time)
3. You'll receive a license key via email

Your license key looks like: `XXXXXXXX-XXXXXXXX-XXXXXXXX-XXXXXXXX`

## Activate Your License

1. Open **Settings → MeetingMind**
2. Scroll to the **License** section
3. Paste your license key
4. Click **Activate**

You should see: **✓ Pro License (Active)**

## Verify Activation

After activation:
- The AI Enrichment section will be unlocked
- You can configure your API key
- AI features will work on new imports

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid license key"

- Check for extra spaces when copying
- Ensure the full key is pasted (4 groups of 8 characters)
- Verify your purchase was completed

### License not activating

- Check your internet connection (validation requires network)
- Try again in a few minutes
- Contact support if issues persist

### Transferring to another device

Your license key works on any device. Simply enter the same key in MeetingMind settings.
